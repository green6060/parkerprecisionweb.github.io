About Me

Ryan Sellers

Born and raised in Washington state, Ryan moved to Utah at age 21 to go to school for music production. After graduating, he started learning more about video and social media.

He started his own videography business in January of 2017. Now he currently runs Aurove Films along side his YouTube channel, Ryan Sellers.
